Anime Descriptions

Frieren

Frieren is a calm fantasy anime that focuses on time, memories, and the meaning of life after an epic hero’s journey ends.

Lucky Star

Lucky Star is a slice-of-life comedy anime that humorously depicts daily school life and otaku culture.

Made in Abyss

Made in Abyss is a dark fantasy adventure anime set in a mysterious and dangerous abyss filled with beauty and horror.
